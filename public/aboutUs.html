About Us

Hi there, my name is Sukanta Dutta , I am a student of Heritage Institute of Technology Kolkata,pursuing Electronics and communication engineering, currently in 3rd year,This is my third webpage as a Major project from Internselite!

Welcome to Sukuz Shop, an impressive digital creation that stands as a testament to my prowess in web development. As the driving force behind this endeavor, I have harnessed a strategic blend of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, while harnessing the power of Node.js to orchestrate a harmonious synergy between frontend aesthetics and backend functionality. Sukuz Shop, hosted gracefully on render.com, is a culmination of meticulous craftsmanship and technological finesse. It represents the culmination of my expertise in web development, with a strong foundation in Node.js as the backend powerhouse. The architecture of Sukuz Shop is artfully organized, boasting a 'public' folder that houses the meticulously crafted HTML, CSS, and JavaScript files, while the pivotal 'server.js' file takes residence within the 'ecommerce' folder, seamlessly linking the frontend and backend elements. One of the notable features is the ingeniously designed '404' page, meticulously linked through JavaScript. This ensures a graceful redirection in the event of an inadvertent navigation into uncharted territories, thereby enhancing user experience. The 'Add Product' section emerges as a portal of entrepreneurial empowerment. Sellers seamlessly transform their aspirations into tangible products within the digital realm, driven by the dynamic capabilities of Node.js. Embark on a journey through the 'Cart' section, where a simple selection cascades into a meticulously orchestrated checkout page. Through a symphony of JavaScript wizardry, users engage with a seamless fusion of visual appeal and practical functionality. The 'Contact Us' page, powered by the sophisticated prowess of EmailJS, serves as a bridge for communication. A sophisticated email generator not only ensures prompt correspondence but also initiates an automated confirmation process, showcasing a comprehensive grasp of backend and frontend integration. Within the 'Seller' realm, entrepreneurs are invited to unleash their creativity. Through an intuitive form, replete with strategically positioned tags, the stage is set for products to find their curated spaces within a meticulously organized digital marketplace. Central to Sukuz Shop's infrastructure is the implementation of Firebase, a realm where user, seller, product, and checkout data converge. Security is paramount, with encrypted password storage and intelligent validation protocols seamlessly woven into the fabric of the system. The visionary integration of Amazon Web Services' S3 is a testament to my commitment to seamless scalability. Images uploaded by sellers are hosted in this ethereal domain, aligning the frontend aesthetic with the backend's robust storage solution. The 'Checkout' experience culminates with a flourish, punctuated by a confirmation email that dances from the system to the user's inbox, expertly synchronizing backend intricacies with user-centric communication. The symphony of Sukuz Shop is harmonized within 'server.js', where routes seamlessly traverse between frontend and backend, orchestrating data flow and functionality with surgical precision. Sukuz Shop serves as a canvas upon which I've painted a profound technological journey, embracing the art of parallax effects and diverse design styles. While Tailwind remains a horizon to explore, this project epitomizes my commitment to technological growth, underscoring the mastery I've attained in Node.js. My journey with Sukuz Shop represents a milestone, not only amplifying my skills in Node.js but also unveiling a universe of possibilities in web development. This project is a testament to my pursuit of excellence and innovation. With utmost dedication and enthusiasm, Sukanta Dutta
